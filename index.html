My name is Gonzalo Tucker

I have a strong background in Astrophysics and mathematics, which has shaped my analytical approach to data-centric roles. Starting as a lab technician, I quickly transitioned into software engineering and QA testing, before finding my niche in data analysis and production support.

I've implemented solutions for complex monthly reporting, engineered large-scale data adjustments, and played a key role in critical data migration projects. My ability to efficiently manage and resolve over 3000 JIRA tickets demonstrates my problem-solving skills and dedication to ensuring reliable service operations.

I'm particularly proud of my work on a data quality dashboard with the CTO, which was recognized company-wide. This project, along with my efforts in creating custom reports and automations for clients, showcases my ability to leverage data for actionable insights.

In addition to my professional experience, I've been deeply engaged with AI and machine learning technologies. My journey began with deep learning projects and has evolved to working with Large Language Models (LLMs).
